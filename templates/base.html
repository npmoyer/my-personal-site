About Me

I am a highly motivated software engineer with a passion for utilizing technology to improve healthcare. I graduated from The Ohio State University in 2017 with a B.S. in Engineering Physics with a specialization in Computer Science Engineering. For my senior capstone project, I gained valuable experience as a part of a team that designed and built an autonomous sample retrieval robot.

Since graduation, I have gained extensive experience with a wide range of software projects. My years of experience have allowed me to become comfortable working in agile environments, adapting to evolving project requirements with ease. My passion for software engineering is driven by the desire to solve challenging problems and continuously improve my skills and knowledge.

My current focus is leveraging the power of cloud computing and AI, with the goal of transforming the broken healthcare system. My adaptability and willingness to take on multiple roles make me a valuable asset to any team.
